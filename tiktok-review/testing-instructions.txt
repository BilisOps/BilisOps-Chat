(paste into the "Step-by-step Instructions For Product Testing" field)

1. Log in to https://chat.bilisops.com with the test account:
   Email: tester@bilisops.com
   Password: admin123

2. STORE CONNECTION — Open Settings > Store Authorization. The TikTok sandbox
   shop (SANDBOX7657414262957000468) is already connected through the official
   TikTok Shop authorization flow. To see the authorization flow itself, select
   "TikTok" and click "Connect TikTok store" — the seller is redirected to the
   TikTok authorization page; after approval they are redirected back and the
   shop appears in the table. Any number of shops can be connected.

3. UNIFIED CHAT INBOX — Open Chats (bottom navigation). Buyer conversations
   from the connected TikTok shop appear in one inbox (webhook push, plus an
   automatic API pull sync every minute). Click the "🧪 Test message" button to
   generate a sample buyer message if the inbox is empty.

4. REPLY TO A BUYER — Open any conversation, type a reply in the composer and
   click Send. Replies to TikTok conversations are delivered to the buyer
   through the TikTok Shop customer service (send message) API.

5. AI-ASSISTED DRAFT — Open a conversation and click "✨ AI Draft". The app
   drafts a suggested reply grounded in the seller's Product Catalog, Reply
   Rules, and Knowledge Pack. The agent reviews/edits the draft before Send —
   the AI never sends on its own.

6. AI CONTROLS — Open AI (bottom navigation):
   • Product Catalog — the products the AI is allowed to talk about (price,
     variants, stock, promos). Try "+ Add product".
   • AI Reply Rules — seller-written rules the AI must follow (e.g. "no
     discounts beyond listed promos"). Try adding/toggling a rule.
   • AI Chatbot — per-shop Knowledge Pack (shipping, returns, policies), with
     a scope selector for account-wide vs. per-shop facts.

7. ANALYTICS — Open Analytics > Sales Conversion to see response rate,
   first-response time, and conversation-to-order metrics computed from the
   inbox and order events.
