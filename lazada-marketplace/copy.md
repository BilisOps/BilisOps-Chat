WHAT IS BILISOPS CHAT?

BilisOps Chat is a live chat management workspace made for Lazada sellers in the Philippines. It puts every buyer conversation from your store(s) into one clean inbox, so you and your team reply faster, close more orders, and keep your response rate high.

KEY FEATURES

• Unified inbox — all buyer chats from your authorized Lazada store(s) in one screen, with unread highlights so nothing slips.
• AI-assisted replies — one click drafts an answer grounded in YOUR product catalog, prices, promos, and policies. You review and send, or enable full auto-reply for FAQs.
• Quick replies — canned answers your team inserts instantly.
• Buyer panel — the customer's current and previous orders (status, items, amount, tracking) right beside the conversation.
• Multi-store — connect any number of shops; filter every dashboard per store.
• Analytics — response rate, first-response time, conversation-to-order conversion, AI vs agent workload, and per-store health.
• Sensitive-word filter — blocks risky outgoing messages before they send.
• Taglish-ready — the AI matches your buyer's language: English, Tagalog, or Taglish.

HOW IT WORKS

1. Subscribe on the Lazada Service Marketplace.
2. Create your BilisOps account and authorize your store through Lazada's official login — we never see your password, and you can revoke anytime.
3. Chats appear in your inbox; reply manually, with quick replies, or with AI assistance.

We access your store only through Lazada's official Open Platform authorization. Data is isolated per seller, tokens are stored encrypted, and synced data is deleted within 30 days if you disconnect. ## Service instruction (Default / English — rich text)
```
GETTING STARTED WITH BILISOPS CHAT

1. SUBSCRIBE — Purchase or start the free trial of BilisOps Chat here on the Lazada Service Marketplace.

2. CREATE YOUR ACCOUNT — Go to https://chat.bilisops.com and sign up with your email. Your first login creates your workspace.

3. CONNECT YOUR STORE — In the app: Settings → Store Authorization → select Lazada → "Connect Lazada store". Log in with your Lazada seller account and approve. Your shop appears as Authorized. Repeat for additional shops.

4. SET UP THE AI (optional but recommended)
   • AI → Product Catalog: add your products (price, variants, stock, promos) — the AI answers product questions from this list only.
   • AI → AI Reply Rules: write your rules (e.g. "always reply with po/opo", "no discounts beyond listed promos").
   • AI → AI Chatbot → Knowledge Pack: add shipping, returns, and store policies per shop.

5. START CHATTING — Open Chats. Buyer messages appear in the unified inbox. Type a reply, use a quick reply, or press "AI Draft" and approve the suggestion. Enable AI auto-reply if you want BilisBot to answer FAQs on its own.

6. TRACK RESULTS — Home and Analytics show your response rate, first-response time, orders guided by chat, and AI vs agent workload — filterable per store.

NEED HELP? Email bilisops.app@gmail.com and we'll walk you through setup (English/Tagalog).
```
